4. Auto-Renewal, Billing, and Cancellation

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.
  • Your subscription renews automatically unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • Your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
